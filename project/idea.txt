⭐ 1. Borrow Anything – “Rent From Neighbour” Platform
✔ Real-life Problem

People often need things only for 1–2 days (drill machine, ladder, camera, cooking vessel), but shops don’t rent it or it's expensive.

✔ Solution (Website Idea)

A community-based platform where neighbours can rent items to each other.

Features

Live map of nearby rentable items

Chat with owner

Digital rental agreement

Deposit security

Damage report system
